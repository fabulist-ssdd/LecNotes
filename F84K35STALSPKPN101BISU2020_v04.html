1. Sources of Data

数据来源（Data Sources）的考核要点主要就是举例。大部分情况下是将各种数据来源列出来，要求判断 各自是什么类型的数据来源。我们的课程当中讲述了 3 种不同的数据来源：Primary Source，Secondary Source, 以及 Tertiary Source。如果题目当中出现了多种数据来源的举例的判断，那么推荐采用排除法。 首先判断最简单的 Tertiary Source，一般会用 Wikipedia 作为例子。然后判断 Secondary Source。找到 一切来自于公司，组织，政府机构，报纸杂志期刊等媒体或渠道的数据，都可以被考虑为 Secondary Source。剩下的应该就是 Primary Source 的例子了。那么，同时一定要能分清楚 Primary 以及 Secondary 的区别。具体可以参考相应课件。关于数据来源更多的举例，请参考本章节以下内容。

2. Data Collecting Methods

  • 数据收集方式（Data Collecting Methods）的考核要点是根据相应案例识别恰当的数据收集方式，并且解释 相应选择的原因。

  • 在识别方面有可能利用选择题或者简答题来进行考核。选择题一般情况下都是单选题。简答题是有能 出现开放式答案的。那么，后文当中已经提供了很多各种数据收集方式的案例。请一定 好好复习。虽然复习材料当中并没有考试原题，但是几乎所有案例都是大同小异的。

  • 关于解释方面，基本上是用简答题来考核的，个别情况下出现选择题，但是在已经确定数据收集方式 的情况下，也只有可能是单选题。后面的小章节当中提供了针对每一种数据收集方式的特点的解释，可 作为答题时组织答案的参考信息。关于选择数据收集方式的解释，简答时一定要结合题干当中所给出的 案例的特征，结合你所选择的数据类型的特点来进行回答，否则不得分。

2.1. Observation

Observation 的特点包括如下几点：

  • collect data via collector’s vision
    数据是通过收集者的视觉来进行的。

  • applied while respondents cannot response to normal request
    该方法仅在被调查者无法对正常的询问作出任何形式的反馈的时候。

  • can be assistant by technical equipment
    收集时可以借助技术仪器的辅助或支持。

Example 5. Example of Observation

The manager would like to know that when, how many people choose certain meals in a restaurant. This information will be useful to the manager in the provision that they make for customers.

Example 6. Example of Observation

The school manager would like to investigate how many student play cellphone game during the class session. This information could help them to regulate students’ behaviours.

Example 7. Example of Observation

Example 8. Example of Observation

Example 9. Example of Observation

Investigating the number of customers visiting a supermarket between 1pm and 3pm on a Monday

Example 10. Example of Observation

A network engineer is testing the Wi-Fi signal in a airport lobby. The signal strengthens measure will be conducted via transmission testing of a single package that ping to the target network access point, and the process will be monitored and recorded by a meter which will indicate the response time of the package transmission by second.

2.2. Questionnaire

Questionnaire 的特点包括如下几点：

  • targeting large group of population and wide geographical coverage
    应对人数众多的被调查群体，以及广泛的地理覆盖范围。

  • data is collected via a series of pre-designed questions.
    数据是通过一系列提前设计好的问题来实现的。

  • respondents only need to ﬁll in the questionnaire once.
    被调查者只需要填写一次问卷。

Example 11. Example of Questionnaire

A survey to be carried out on the smoking habits of employees of a local college

Example 12. Example of Questionnaire

A gas company enquiring into customer satisfaction.

Example 13. Example of Questionnaire

Obtaining opinions about the facilities available at a local sports centre.

2.3. Interview

Interview 的特点包括如下几点：

  • collect data via face-to-face conversation
    通过面对面的交谈来收集数据。

  • suitable on collecting personal opinions and sensitive data, in-depth
    适合深入的了解收集个人见解以及具有敏感性的数据。

  • targeting relatively smaller group of respondents
    仅适用于小范围的调查。

Example 14. Example of Interview

Getting an in depth insight into the experiences of war veterans.

Example 15. Example of Interview

A business company is trying to gather the information of working experiences from its employees.

Example 16. Example of Interview

In order to build a new effective project team, during the ﬁnal round of selection, manager would like to meet applicants who have survived multiple exams in person, which allows him to understand these applicants, and maybe gather some information regarding their personal status.

Example 17. Example of Interview

Journalists gathering information on the eve of an election

2.4. Respondents' Schedule

Respondents’ Schedule 的特点包括如下几点：

  • collect data via respondent’s logging activities
    数据通过被调查者自己的日志行为来收集。

  • logging need to be recorded frequently and periodically
    日志的生成具有重复性和循环型。

  • the records of logs will be collected by researcher following a pre-designed timeframe
    所有日志将会根据提前设计好的时间框架来由研究者收集。

Example 18. Example of Respondent’s Schedule

For the purpose of providing better facility in employee resting room, company carries a survey on employees’ behaviour pattern in the current resting room. researcher has designed a particular log for each employee to ﬁll their use of the facilities and their opinions over a year.

Example 20. Example of Respondent’s Schedule

3. Types of Data

数据类型（Data Types）的考试重点在定义以及举例:

  • 定义方面的考核有可能是选择题或者是简答题。简答题方面通常在题干当中给出数据类型的名称，要 求答出该数据类型的定义，或者用恰当的语言描述。选择题方面可能给出某种数据类型的描述，要求在 众多选项当中挑选出最合适的数据类型名称。

  • 在下面的小章节当中对每一种数据类型都做了一定的解释并且提供了至少一种定义文本。首先，一定要 背。其次，一定更要搞清楚 Qualitative Data 以及 Quantitative Data 两者的区别，以及各自的子类数据 类型都包含什么。Attribute Data 的定义范围是完全独立于前两者的，需要在理解前两者定义的情况下 才好记忆。

  • 举例方面的考核如果是简答题的话，通常就是直接在题干当中给出数据类型的名称，要求答出 1 到 2 种 恰当的例子。这样的题型有可能跟考定义的简答题结合在一起。如果是选择题的话，类型就比较丰富了。 例如，提供数据类型的名称要求选择恰当的例子，或者提供例子要求选择恰当的数据类型名称。甚至有 可能跟数据收集方式以及抽样方法的题目结合起来，问相应案例当中描述的是什么类型的数据。

关于数据类型的举例请参考后文当中的相应部分。

3.1. Qualitative Data

Qualitative Data 定性数据也叫做 Categorical Data 或者 Descriptive Data。下面方框当中提供了 3 种 不同的定义，请一定背下其中任意一种。

Qualitative data are normally measures of ’types’ and may be represented by a name, symbol, or number code.

Qualitative data is data that is descriptive by variable’s nature.

Qualitative data is the variables associated with values that can be placed into categories.

3.1.1. Subtypes of Qualitative Data

Ordinal Data

Ordinal Data 有序数据是 Qualitative Data 的一个子类。下面方框当中提供了 2 种不同的定义，请一定 背下其中任意一种。

Ordinal data is a type of qualitative data type consisting of ranking or order.

Ordinal data are qualitative data where there is logical ordering to the categories.

Nominal Data

Nominal Data 无序数据是 Qualitative Data 的一个子类。下面方框当中提供了 3 种不同的定义，请一定 背下其中任意一种。

Nominal data are qualitative variables which are differentiated by a simple naming system.

Nominal data is usually categorical, in that they belong to a definable category.

Nominal data differentiates between items or subjects based only on their names or categories and other qualitative classifications they belong to.

Quantitative data is the variables associated with values that represent quantities.

Continuous data is the numerical variables that can assume any value within the observed range.

3.3. Attribute Data

Attribute Data 属性数据是一种比较特殊的数据类型。当以判断某种特性或者属性为目的的情况下，能 够将定性数据通过计数的形式转变为离散值的时候，该定性数据则为属性数据。下面的方框当中提供了 两种不同的属性数据的定义，请一定背诵下来任意一种。

Attribute data can be represented in describe units which is measured with a responsive variable.

Attribute data is qualitative data which be able to be counted in discrete units that measures an attribute of a product specification.

4. Data Sampling Methods

  • 数据抽样方式（Data Collecting Methods）的考核要点是根据相应案例识别恰当的数据抽样方式，并且解释相应选择的原因。

  • 在识别方面有可能利用选择题或者简答题来进行考核。选择题一般情况下都是单选题。简答题一般不 会出现开放式答案。有可能在题干当中描述一个案例，问题要求提供最合适的数据抽样方式。也有可能在题干的案例当中已经描述了一种数据抽样方式，问题要求识别出来。那么，在复习过程当中需要参考更多各种数据收集方式的案例的话，请查询后文相应部分。虽然复习材料当中并没有考试原题，但是 几乎所有案例都是大同小异的。

  • 关于解释方面，基本上是用简答题来考核的，个别情况下出现选择题，但是在已经确定数据抽样方式 的情况下，也只有可能是单选题。后面的小章节当中提供了针对每一种数据抽样方式的特点的解释，可作为答题时组织答案的参考信息。关于选择数据抽样方式的解释，简答时一定要结合题干当中所给出的 案例的特征，结合你所选择的数据类型的特点来进行回答，否则不得分。

4.1. Simple Random Sampling

Simple Random Sampling 的特点包括如下几点：

  • aim to ensure the equal probability of selection of each data items
    目的是为了确保每个数据个体拥有同等被选中的可能性。

  • must have a sample frame, and the frame is not subdivided or partitioned
    必须拥有抽样框架，并且该框架没有被分类。

  • must have a fixed given size to the sampling group
    必须固定每次抽样的样本数量。

Example 26. Examples of Simple Random Sampling

A school manager is trying to upgrade the public sport facility in the school campus. he has obtained massive amount of survey data basically from all students and faculties in school via questionnaire. Now, he needs to select samples from this collection, and he would like to ensure every single questionnaire can have an equal chance to be selected.

Example 27. Examples of Simple Random Sampling

Company A wants to choose ﬁve people from its 100 customers to take a vacation in New Zealand. ensure each person on the list has an equal chance of selection.

4.2. Systematic Sampling

Systematic Sampling 的特点包括如下几点：

  • relies on arranging the target population according to some ordering scheme and then selecting elements at regular intervals through that ordered list
    首先将被调查群体按照某种规则进行排序，之后在该循序的指导下，按照固定的间隔进行样本选取。

  • deals with infinite population without fixed frame
    适合应对没有固定抽样框架的无限数据。

  • the start point of sampling need to be selected randomly, and the gap between 2 nearby sampling points need to randomly regulated
    抽样的开始点需要随机选取，两个相邻抽样点之间的间隔需要随机规定。

Example 28. Examples of Systematic Sampling

A quality inspector needs to carry out his daily check on the computer parts product in the workshop. the production line is a configuration based upon individual assembling worker. the inspector need to randomly select one worker, then collect the finished products from 2 nearby workers by every 5 workers.

Example 29. Examples of Systematic Sampling

A motherboard manufacturer is trying to figure out the most appropriate method of quality control. the chipset making process is current carry out by an automatic assembling line which control by computer, and the line is operating 24 hours. the key point of quality control to check whether chipset can be installed with correct unit and in right location. which method of sampling would be the most appropriate to ensure consistency of the motherboard quality throughout the assembling process?

Example 30. Examples of Systematic Sampling

In a railway construction site, workers have been install the marking pole for distance identification. the major responsibility of quality inspector is to ensure the consistency of distance marking, which is 10 meters. which method of sampling would be the most appropriate to ensure consistency of the distance between 2 marking poles?

4.3. Stratified Sampling

Stratiﬁed Sampling 的特点包括如下几点：

  • suitable for heterogeneity dataset (the sample frame can be subdivided or partitioned)
    适合异质性数组（抽样框架可被分组）

  • the researcher divides the entire population into different subgroups, then, randomly selects the ﬁnal subjects from the different subgroups with same group size.
    研究者首先将整个被调查群体分成不同的组，然后从每组当中抽取同等数量的数据组成最终的样 例组。

  • better recognition to the features of the overall population, and the difference between the subgroups.
    适合于观测整个被调查群体的特性，以及每个分组之间的不同。

Example 31. Examples of Stratified Sampling

Company BBB employs 1000 people in its Chinese office, 400 people in its Japanese branch, and 600 in its UK office. as an anniversary will be held next month, a sample of 40 employees should be chosen to attend it.

Example 32. Examples of Stratified Sampling

A marketing researcher needs to carry out buying trend study based upon company’s sales record in the previous year. There are several types of products have been recorded in the database. The researcher needs to randomly select sample group from each type of product.

4.4. Quota Sampling

Quota Sampling 的特点包括如下几点：

  • similar to stratified sampling, but the selection of the sample is non-random
    跟 Stratified Sampling 类似，但是样本的选择并不是随机的。

  • the researcher divides the entire population into different subgroups, then the subjects selected dependent on a specified proportion
    研究者首先将整个被调查群体分成不同的组，然后根据特定的比例来选取样本。

  • the principle of dividing the population might not be the features of heterogeneity dataset
    对被调查群体的分组原则可能并不是基于异质性数组的本来特性。

Example 33. Examples of Quota Sampling

A network provider is trying to figure out the current condition of the Internet usage in the city. data is collected via anonymous questionnaires. However, in order to carry out detailed further study, the income level of other subject can be considered as an important measurement. the researcher needs to set a different level f incomes as categories.

4.5. Cluster Sampling

Stratified Sampling 的特点包括如下几点：

  • often applied on research regarding biology, geographic, agriculture and forestry, and similar field, while respondents need to be clustered geographically
    经常应用在生物，地理，农林，等类似领域的研究中，当被调查数据需要通过地理特性来划分时。

  • applied while respondents are spread in wide geographic area with unbalanced distribution, fairly similar between individuals with specific details, and impossible of obtaining a sample frame
    该方法应用的原则为，当被调查群体不均等的分布在较为广泛的地理覆盖范围内，个体与个体之 间拥有相当的近似程度，不能获取抽样框架。

  • the research need to separate the entire investigation region into several smaller manageable areas, then randomly select areas from grid system for investigation activities, and finally, each selected area shall be investigated thoroughly
    研究者需要首先将待调查区域划分成一些相对较小的易于管理的区域，然后随机选取一些区域作为调查活动的执行地点，最后所有被选择到的区域应该被彻底的调查。

Example 34. Examples of Cluster Sampling

In order to study the greening condition of a mega city, researchers have constructed a grid system to separated the entire city into several areas. then, they need to randomly select 10 areas to carry out the detail study, as they have only 10 groups currently.

Example 35. Examples of Cluster Sampling

A biological institution wants to investigate the number of worms on a sand beach

Example 36. Examples of Cluster Sampling

A network engineer is testing the Wi-Fi signal in an airport lobby. He needs to select testing location for signal strengthen testing. Based upon the standard procedure, he need to separate the entire lobby into a grid based upon the current distribution of network access points, and run completed testing on those blocks selected by random pick.

4.6. Multi-Stage Sampling

Multi-stage Sampling 的特点包括如下几点：

  • considered as a complex form of cluster sampling
    通常认为这是 Cluster Sampling 的一种复杂形式

  • useful while targeting massive amount of data items
    在应对极大量的数据时非常有用。

  • the investigation regions will be clustered and randomly selected by several stages, until the necessary and acceptable details of data can be obtained
    待调查区域将被按不同的级别进行多次的切分和随机选取，直到能获取到必要的且可接受的数据 细节为止。

Example 37. Examples of Multi-Stage Sampling

Researcher investigate the process of new vaccine injection in the whole county. Basically, researcher need to randomly sample in each level of geographical category in order to minimise the sampling area and ensure the final selection is applicable for data collecting. It is possible to randomly select province, than randomly pick city/town from province, then, randomly select district from city/town, the final stage can be hospital.
